About Us

KeiMachines

M/s. Kamal Industries is a proprietary company engaged in the manufacture of machinery for the processing and packaging of Pharmaceuticals, Foods, Chemicals and Cosmetics. Established more than 3 decades ago, the Company has been serving the industry with good quality products & timely service. The range of products are exported to Australia, the Pacific Islands, Malaysia, Kenya, Nigeria, Uganda, Sudan, Zambia, Ghana, Uganda, Tanzania, The Middle East & the neighbouring SAARC countries. The machines are manufactured for both Production or Laboratory purposes and are available with options of operation in manual, semi-automatic & automatic modes. and in both stand alone and automated lines.
